Privacy Policy

Monify - Personal Finance Tracking App

Summary: Monify stores your data locally on your device. Your financial information is not sent to our servers and is not shared with third parties.

1. Data We Collect

Monify collects and stores the following data only on your device:

  • Income and expense records (amount, category, date, notes)
  • Categories you create
  • App preferences (theme, currency, language)

2. Data Storage

All your financial data is stored in your device's local storage (Hive database). This data:

  • Is not transmitted over the internet
  • Is not stored on our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is completely removed when you delete the app

3. Third-Party Services

Monify uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Used for subscription management. RevenueCat processes anonymous user IDs and purchase information. For details: RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. For details: Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

For the security of your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Your device's security features (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode) protect your data
  • The app does not request unnecessary permissions

5. Children's Privacy

Monify does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. You will be notified through the app if significant changes occur.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • View your data (within the app)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app or within the app)
  • Export your data (CSV export feature)

8. Contact

For questions about our privacy policy:

Email: support@monify.app
